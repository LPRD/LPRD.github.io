What does LPRD stand for (literally)?

(pronounced leopard): Liquid Propelled Rocket Design

Okay... And what does LPRD do, exactly?

We Like Rockets

Our rocketry organization, run primarily by students at the University of Minnesota, might have changed in its look and membership over the years, but its mission remains the same:

develop a liquid propellant rocket capable of reaching the edge of space.

Teamwork Environment

Our team gains technical and leadership skills that bolster the classroom experience through practical design, manufacture, and testing of its own engines, as well as allowing students to manage projects under the supervision of advisors and faculty.

The University of Minnesota

We're always looking for new members and mentors to lead LPRD's next generation of aerospace innovation, and could not have made it as far as we have without the support of our advising staff and the University of Minnesota.
