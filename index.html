Who is LPRD?

We are a grassroots rocketry organization run by students at the University of Minnesota. Our team has changed over the years, but the mission remains the same: develop a liquid propellant rocket capable of reaching outer space. Our team gains valuable technical and leadership skills beyond our classroom experience through the designing, manufacturing, and testing our own engines. We are always looking for new members and mentors to lead LPRD's next generation of aerospace innovation.
