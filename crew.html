Meet the Teams

To promote efficiency, LPRD has four funtional groups:

Propulsion

Among other tasks, this team designs, prototypes, and tests engines and fuel lines.

Avionics

Electronics, especially those required for live telemetry, fall to this team. If any system has a flaw, they maintain logs that allow us to figure out what that flaw is.

Flight

Construction of the rocket body and on-site repairs fall to this team.

Execution

Composed of officers, this team delegates tasks, garners funding, and makes sure that we comply with University of Minnesota and HeroX policy.
