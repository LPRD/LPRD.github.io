Chronology

Here are the HeroX deadlines thrown into a format subtly indicates our annual goals.


 


 

2018

Preliminaries

6 June

The Base 11 Space Challenge kicks off.

2019

Prototyping

22 March

HeroX Deadline: Phase 1 Preliminary Design Report Due. Our team needs to have completed a high-level design of its entire vehicle, with data (be it calculation, analysis, simulation and/or initial test data) to support the reliability of all major subsystems. LPRD must also designate a Safety Officer to maintain its Safety Log.

25 May

HeroX announces the winners of Phase 1 based upon a rubric. Also, HeroX will host a static test firing demonstration and safety session.

8 Nov

HeroX deadline: Phase 2 Interim Report Due. Our team must submit a report documenting the fruits of its cyclically generating new prototypes, evaluating test results, and prototyping all over again.

2020

Design Submission

20 Mar

HeroX deadline: Phase 2 Critical Design Report Due. If we submit this report in advance, we can expect judge feedback at the May 2020 Base 11 Aerospace Symposium.

22 May

HeroX deadline: Static Test Firing Award Submissions Due. Prior to the static test firing, we must submit for approval a design that meets the static test firing guidelines. Following authorization from HeroX, we must perform a test firing and submit a report by this date.

30 May

HeroX: First Launch Window. In order to attempt a launch, we must have successfully submitted both Phase 1 and 2 Reports, as well as our Phase 3 Launch Readiness Report (which includes all design files and safety logs). We must submit Phase 3 documents at least 90 days before the launch window. At this time, judges will either authorize or deny the proposed launch..

5 Dec

HeroX: Second Launch Window. Judges will either authorize or deny the proposed launch by the same criteria as in the first launch window.

2021

Approval and Launch

15 May

HeroX: Third Launch Window. Judges will either authorize or deny the proposed launch by the same criteria as in the first launch window.

11 Dec

HeroX: Last Launch Window. In order to attempt a launch at the final launch window, we must submit Phase 3 documents by June 18, 2021. Judges will provide written feedback by August 13, and will provide review and discussion in early September. Launch approval or denial will arrive by early October.
